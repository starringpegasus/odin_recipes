Description

An easy-to-make taco recipe that can be enjoyed by taco lovers all around, this ground beef recipe is sure to delight your tastebuds. It is flavorful and criminally underrated as a favorite food. Simple and easy, anyone can follow along!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Chili powder
  • Cumin
  • Cumin
  • Garlic powder
  • Mexican oregano
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Place ground beef in skillet with olive oil.
  2. Brown beef with oil and drain the fat.
  3. Add spices, tomato paste, and water.
  4. Bring mixture to a simmer and wait until sauce thickens.
  5. Done! Enjoy your meal.
