Cheeseburger Recipe

Home

Description

This delicious cheeseburger recipe resembles the perfectly curated "Texas Roadhouse" cheeseburger. It is salty, juicy, and sure to be enjoyed by all.

Ingredients

  • Beef chuck, freshly ground and loose pack
  • Bbq sauce of choice
  • Vegtable oil
  • Hamburger buns
  • American cheese
  • Sweet onion
  • Dill pickle slices

Steps

  1. Fluff the freshly ground beef chuck
  2. Lightly salt and pepper your beef and fluff again
  3. Divide your meat into four equal portions
  4. Make your pattys
  5. Heat your electric skillet to 400 degrees
  6. Add oil and swish so the bottom of the skillet is covered
  7. Carefully transfer the pattys to the skillet
  8. Flatten the patties with a spatula
  9. Cook for 4-5 minutes, then flip once nice and brown
  10. Cook for an additonal 4 minutes
  11. Remove the pattys
  12. Brush with barbeque sauce and add toppings
  13. >Put on the buns, and enjoy! Can serve with fries.
