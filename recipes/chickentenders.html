Chicken Tender Recipe

Home

Description

This fantastic recipe will teach you how to make chicken tenders at home.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 pounds chicken tenders
  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flower
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg beaten with 2 tablespoons of water
  • Vegtable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Fill a 9-inch cast iron or standard skillet with about 2 inches of oil. Start heating it over medium-high heat while you bread the chicken
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, salt and pepper
  3. In another large bowl, beat the egg and water
  4. Dredge the chicken in the flour, coating well. Shake off excess flour and dip in the egg, the back in the flour
  5. Set the chicken to the side to rest for about 5 minutes.
  6. Check your oil temperature with a candy thermometer if necessary
  7. Turn, and repeat until all brown
  8. Cook the rest of the chicken in batches.
  9. Transfer chicken to plate and sprinkle salt. Enjoy!
